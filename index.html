Speaker Lineup

Dr. Isabelle Moreau

A visionary leader in artificial intelligence and ethical technology, Dr. Isabelle Moreau has spent over a decade shaping the future of human-AI collaboration. Based in Paris, she advises global organizations on responsible innovation, bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world applications. Passionate about diversity in tech, she advocates for inclusive AI solutions that benefit society as a whole.

Prof. Alexandre Dubois

A leading expert in sustainable urban development, Prof. Alexandre Dubois specializes in designing eco-friendly cities that balance technology and nature. With over 20 years of research and policy advising, he has contributed to groundbreaking projects that redefine mobility, energy efficiency, and urban resilience. His work in Paris serves as a global model for sustainable metropolitan innovation.

Dr. Julian Carter

Dr. Julian Carter explores how emerging technologies impact human behavior, learning, and decision-making. His work bridges neuroscience and AI, shaping the future of human-machine interactions. As an advocate for ethical AI, he has spoken at major global conferences, emphasizing the need for responsible innovation in the digital age.
